About

Margaretta Colangelo is a native San Franciscan with over 30 years of experience working in the software industry in Silicon Valley. Margaretta is focused on DeepTech, AI, Longevity, and Enterprise Blockchain. Margaretta has written over 60 articles on AI, and publishes a weekly DeepTech Newsletter with Dmitry Kaminskiy. Margaretta's articles have been published in MIT Technology Review Italia, Pharmaceutical Executive, and Health Management Journal. Interviews with Margaretta have been published in Forbes, Outsourced Pharma, Pharmaceutical Executive, Healthcare IT News, and Il Sole 24 Ore. Margaretta moderates sessions at select AI conferences, and has over 24,000 followers on LinkedIn. She is based in San Francisco.

WORK

Deep Knowledge Ventures has three subsidiaries, Deep Knowledge Analytics, Aging Analytics Agency, and Longevity.Capital which together form the Deep Knowledge Group.
