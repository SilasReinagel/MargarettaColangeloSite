Work

Deep Knowledge Group is an international consortium of commercial and non-profit organizations focused on DeepTech and Frontier Technologies. Our subsidiaries and associated organizations include Deep Knowledge Ventures, Longevity Capital, AI Pharma Capital, Longevity Bank, Longevity Card, Longevity FinTech Company, Deep Knowledge Analytics, NeuroTech Analytics, Aging Analytics Agency, Biogerontology Research Foundation, Longevity Swiss Foundation, Longevity International UK - Secretariat for the UK All-Party Parliamentary on Longevity, and AI-Longevity Consortium at King’s College London. Our DeepTech investment fund, Deep Knowledge Ventures, is focused on AI, Biopharma, and Longevity. We have offices in Hong Kong, Honolulu, San Francisco, London, and Geneva.

Jthereum is an enterprise Blockchain technology company that transforms Java developers into Blockchain developers. With Jthereum, 10 million Java developers around the world can write smart contracts and interact with existing contracts in Java, without having to learn Solidity.

Longevity Capital Fund is a specialized hybrid fund based in Switzerland that combines the profitability of venture funds with the liquidity of hedge funds.

AI Pharma Capital Fund is a hybrid investment fund specifically focused on the AI in Pharma and Drug Discovery sector.

Longevity Bank is the first bank to on-board AI, Data Science, AgeTech, and HealthTech to develop new financial products for clients who are planning to live extra long lives and want to remain high functioning and financially stable throughout.

The Longevity FinTech Company based in London develops FinTech 2.0 tools and solutions for the Longevity FinTech framework.

NeuroTech Analytics is the world’s premier source of NeuroTech Industry intelligence, forecasting and benchmarking, and the central repository of NeuroTech market insights.

Deep Knowledge Analytics is a leading DeepTech analytical agency focused on AI and the convergence of technological MegaTrends.

Aging Analytics Agency is the world’s premier provider of industry analytics on Longevity, Precision Preventive Medicine and Economics of Aging.

U1 Technologies provides the communications infrastructure used at the core of stock trading applications by the world's top multi-national investment banks.

The AI PHI at the University of Hawaii Cancer Center is the world's first AI Precision Health Institute using AI to assess health and predict risk of disease.

The Longevity AI Consortium at King's College London is the first AI for Longevity Hub in Europe. The Consortium will expand to include centers in Switzerland, Israel, Singapore, and the United States through a £7 million commitment from Deep Knowledge Ventures.

First Longevity is a media and finance business focused on the growing longevity market opportunity.

Robots Go Mental develops Transfer Learning on Steroids (TLoS). With this technology deep networks can learn much more efficiently than by using traditional methods.

Gennev is the first teleheath platform designed for women over 40.

Bring Hope Humanitarian Foundation works to alleviate the suffering of those affected by war and poverty.
